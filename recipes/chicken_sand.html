chicken sandwich

A Crispy Chicken Sandwich with a juicy and tender center. Learn how to make fried chicken breast with a perfectly seasoned thick and shaggy crust.

ingredients

  • large chicken thighs
  • buttermilk
  • salt & pepper
  • hot sauce
  • onion & garlic powder
  • flower
  • baking powder
  • paprika
  • oil
  • buns, buttered and toasted
  • green lettuce leaves
  • large tomato, sliced
  • dill pickles
  • mayo or your favorite sauce!

Steps

  1. Tenderize and trim chicken thighs, flatten to cook evenly.
  2. Combine buttermilk, hot sauce, salt & pepper, onion & garlic. Add chicken thighs and optionally let marinate up to overnight.
  3. In a separate bowl whisk together breading ingredients: flour, salt & pepper, baking powder, paprika, onion & garlic powder.
  4. Dip chicken into breading mixture until it is well coated then transfer to wire rack.
  5. Heat 1/4" oil in a tall pan, just before frying, dip chicken into breading for extra thick exterior. Add chicken slowly and do not crowd pan.
  6. Cook 3-4 minutes on the first side or until browned and crisp then flip and cook another 2-3 minutes on the second side or until cooked through and chicken is browned and crisp all over.
  7. Transfer to a wire rack set over a baking sheet. Lightly butter and toast the cut sides of your buns and build your crispy chicken sandwiches with desired toppings.
